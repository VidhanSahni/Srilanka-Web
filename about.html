Who We Are?

visitsrilanka.live is your go-to resource for planning a trip to Sri Lanka in 2025 which is a product of a student who is in (PIS) Pride International School, under the web application development module.
Our website features a wide range of information on the top attractions, adventure activities, upcoming events, a blog of popular tourist destinations, and traditional products such as masks, spices, arts, foods, industrial goods... etc. Whether you're looking to relax on beautiful beaches, explore ancient temples, or discover the local cuisine, visitsrilanka.live has you covered. What is PIS?

Pride International School, Pride International School, Narkatiaganj (A unit of Kamala Smriti Trust) is one of the unique, best CBSE, English Medium a co-ed school in the heart of our town. It is a private English Medium School, established to render the best education, child can receive. Pride International School has a team of experienced Management, which has renowned CEO's and Vice Presidents of Multinational Companies (MNC's) from Bangarulu, USA, UK, and Australia. They make up the PRIDES of our Pride International School.Pride International School prepares students for Debates, Elocution and many more. They learn the art of Public Speaking in school. Not only this but Drama, Music, Dance, Art and Craft and many such more activities ate very popular at Pride International School, where students Enthusiastically take part in Inter-House Competitions.For Boarding School Students of classes 3 to 8, there are Dormitories that allow students to be under the Direct Supervision of Wardens. The school provides a single bed to each kid along with an Individual Locker. Each Dormitory has its own toilet and shower block.Our Expert Faculties are our Assets. Our teachers create a Vibrant Academic Atmosphere for all students.
